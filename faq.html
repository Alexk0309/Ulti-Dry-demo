FAQ

How much is the shipping fee?

The shipping fee spans from a range of RM2 - RM6 based on your current location’s distance from our storage centre in Subang Jaya

Do you sell your products overseas?

Unfortunately, we currently do not ship our products overseas as well as East Malaysia. However, as our business continues to expand, we will continue to expand our shipping horizons to reach more people!

Does Ulti-Dry have any physical retail stores?

For now, Ulti-Dry does not have any physical stores. Follow us on our social media to keep up to date with any updates coming from us

Does Ulti-Dry sell products for kids?

No, Ulti-Dry does not currently sell children’s clothing products. However, that could change if we see a high demand for those products among our customers.
